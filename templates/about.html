Biography

Learn more about the artist and the collection.

Overview

This collection explores form, light, and narrative through a contemporary lens. Works span studies, finished pieces, and commissioned projects.

Materials include ink, acrylic, and mixed media on various substrates.

Early Years

The artist began with observational sketches that evolved into larger narrative scenes. Influences include classical composition and street photography.

Exhibitions and workshops shaped the practice and technique.

Collections

Featured series include cityscapes, abstract studies, and portrait narratives. Each series explores a distinct palette and rhythm.

Limited editions and originals are cataloged and archived for provenance.
